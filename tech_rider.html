General Information

This technical rider outlines all requirements for successful event execution. Please review all sections carefully and ensure all specifications are met prior to the event date.

DJ Equipment Requirements

DJ Personal Equipment

  • DJ headphones (Sennheiser HD-25 or equivalent)
  • USB drives with music library (backup required)
  • Laptop with Serato DJ Pro or Rekordbox

Venue Provided Equipment

  • Professional DJ setup: 2x Pioneer CDJ-2000 or equivalent
  • Professional mixer: Pioneer DJM-900NXS2 or equivalent
  • Quality PA system with subwoofers
  • Monitor speakers for DJ booth
  • Essential cables and connections

DJ Booth & Lighting

DJ Booth Setup

  • DJ table/booth: 6' x 3' (1.8m x 0.9m) minimum
  • Table height: 3.5-4 feet (1.1-1.2m)
  • Stable, non-slip surface
  • Access to power outlets (minimum 4 outlets)
  • Proper cable management system
  • Storage space for equipment cases

Lighting & Effects

  • Minimum 6x LED PAR cans for ambient lighting
  • 4x moving head lights for dynamic effects
  • 2x laser systems (if venue allows)
  • Fog/haze machine (if permitted)
  • DMX control system for lighting synchronization
  • Blackout capability for dramatic effects

Essential Venue Requirements

DJ Booth & Setup

  • DJ table or booth (any size, just stable)
  • Power outlets for laptop and equipment
  • Basic lighting for DJ area
  • Space for personal equipment
  • Access to venue WiFi (if available)

Venue Specifications

  • Space for dancing and audience
  • Basic climate control (not too hot/cold)
  • Access to venue (stairs/elevator ok)
  • Basic storage area for equipment
  • Private area for DJ to prepare (optional)
  • WiFi access (if available)

Power Requirements

  • Power outlets for DJ equipment
  • Power for sound system
  • Basic lighting power
  • Extension cords (if needed)
  • USB charging for laptop/phone (if possible)

Transportation & Logistics

Transportation Requirements

  • DJ travels by train - pickup required from nearest train station
  • Train station pickup and drop-off service required
  • Equipment transport vehicle with proper securing
  • Loading assistance at train station and venue
  • Return transportation to train station after event
  • Backup transportation plan in case of train delays
  • Luggage space for DJ equipment and personal items

Technical Staff & Setup

Helpful Personnel

  • Someone to help with setup (if possible)
  • Venue contact person
  • Basic security (if needed)
  • Help with equipment loading (if needed)

Setup Requirements

  • 1-2 hours setup time (flexible)
  • 30 minutes sound check
  • 30 minutes teardown time
  • DJ arrives 1 hour before performance
  • Basic equipment testing

Safety & Security

Basic Safety Requirements

  • Safe electrical setup
  • Clear access to exits
  • Basic first aid available
  • Safe venue environment
  • Emergency contact information
